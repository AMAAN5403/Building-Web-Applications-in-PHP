Requirnments

  1. You must have at least one example of each of the following tags: span, p, div, h1, and ul
  2. You must have three a tags and three li tags.
  3. You must have some bold text in the document and some italics text in the document but you cannot use the b or i tags. The autograder will only grade HTML, and ignore any CSS.
